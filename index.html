About me

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Zürich, with a background in clinical operations, public health research, and data analysis. I hold a Master of Science in Epidemiology and a Bachelor of Science in Health Science. My research interests include evidence synthesis, research methodology, pragmatic RCT, causal inference, RWD, AI tools, and policy.

Course

An 8‑week hands-on course covering LLM fundamentals, text and data workflows, literature synthesis, custom chatbots and retrieval-augmented systems, plus automation for documents and spreadsheets. Students learn how to automate and enhance their workflows using various AI tools. Topics include document automation, literature reviews, chatbot creation, and visual generation. Tools used include ChatGPT, Julius, Windsurf, Elicit, LiteRev, and Botpress.
